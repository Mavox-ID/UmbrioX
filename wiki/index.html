UmbrioX

Overview

UmbrioX (UBX, ᪣) is a cryptocurrency launched in 2025 by the B*HD™ team on the The Open Network (TON) blockchain. The project focuses on fast, secure transactions with low fees. UmbrioX uses a token-burning mechanism known as the Token Burn System (TBS) to regulate token value. Development is led by the B*HD™ team with support from the UmbrioX Chat community. Official site

History

UmbrioX was announced in 2025 by the B*HD™ team as a cryptocurrency to explore financial opportunities on the TON blockchain. The project draws inspiration from decentralized finance (DeFi) concepts and the Ethereum model. The B*HD™ team, consisting of programmers, designers, and blockchain specialists, developed the UBX token and integrated it with platforms like Blum and DeDust for trading and price analysis.

Technical Specifications

UmbrioX operates on the TON blockchain, offering high throughput and low transaction fees. The UBX token is implemented as a Jetton (TON token standard), though full technical details remain undisclosed. The B*HD™ team claims support for quantum-resistant security and scalability, but these claims require independent verification.

The official website provides real-time UBX pricing via DeDust and tonapi.io APIs. Users can monitor token value and receive price change alerts.

Economic Model

The key feature of UmbrioX is the Token Burn System (TBS), developed by B*HD™. Users send TON tokens to the following address:

UQD7BXjUuq70_Q6-oZARwqxJ0b8GikkTyia8jT2-upQTKfvh

Burning TON tokens ≥1% of the threshold increases UBX value by 0.5% for 24 hours. For example, burning 2 TON or two transactions of 1 TON each activates this effect. The increase resets to 0% after 24 hours if no further burns occur. TBS Details

TBS creates a deflationary model, encouraging ecosystem participation. Burning is voluntary, and users are responsible for their actions.

Partnerships and Integrations

UmbrioX collaborates with projects in the TON ecosystem:

  • Blum — platform for trading and promoting UBX.
  • DeDust — decentralized exchange.
  • tonapi.io — price and transaction data.
  • TON Network — core blockchain platform.

Services like API.Service, Workers, and TBS.Service are mentioned, but their roles are unspecified.

Reviews and Criticism

As of June 2025, independent reviews of UmbrioX are limited due to its early development stage. The UmbrioX Chat community actively supports the project, but further research is needed to assess its long-term viability.
